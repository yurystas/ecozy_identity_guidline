# 13. DSGVO (source) (source2)

**Text:** eCozy GmbH, as a company based in Munich, Germany, operates in strict compliance with the **Datenschutz-Grundverordnung (DSGVO)**, or General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

* **Lawful Basis for Processing:** We only process personal data when we have a lawful basis to do so (e.g., user consent, or for the fulfillment of a contract).  
* **Data Minimization:** We collect only the minimum amount of personal data necessary to provide our smart heating services.  
* **User Rights:** We fully support a user's right to access, rectify, erase ("right to be forgotten"), and port their personal data. These requests can be initiated via our support channels.  
* **Data Processing Agreements (DPAs):** We maintain strict DPAs with all third-party vendors (like AWS) who may process data on our behalf, ensuring they also meet GDPR standards.
