# Brand Policies

This document brings together eCozy's mission, vision, core values, and practical brand principles. It is written to be clear and actionable for product teams, designers, engineers, partners, and content creators.

---

## Mission

Our mission is to provide the world with the most advanced smart heating and home climate solutions. We design innovative, standards-driven products that depart from traditional heating models and common smart-home approaches. Through engineering and design excellence, we aim to change how people use energy and resources to better fit the needs of a rapidly evolving world.

---

## Vision

eCozy envisions creating products and platforms that set new standards within the IoT ecosystem and drive the evolution of the smart home experience. We aspire to be recognized for solutions that are technological, energy-efficient, and delightful to use — enabling smarter, greener homes worldwide.

---

## Core Values

- Preservation — We prioritize sustainable design and responsible use of resources.
- Innovation — We embrace creativity and continuous improvement.
- Adaptation — We design for change: adaptable products, flexible integrations, and future-proof architecture.
- Professionalism — We hold ourselves to high standards of quality and accountability.
- Development-focus — We are committed to continuous learning and engineering excellence.
- Community — We actively support local communities and professional bodies.

---

## Brand Principles (How we express the brand)

### 1. Clear and Honest Communication
- Use straightforward language. Avoid hype and ambiguous claims.
- Present features and benefits with transparent explanations and measurable outcomes.

### 2. Design with Purpose
- Every visual and interaction decision must serve user needs or brand clarity.
- Prioritize readability, accessibility, and consistent spacing.

### 3. Consistent Visual Identity
- Follow the logo, color, and typography rules documented in the Visual Identity section.
- Maintain consistent use of iconography and illustrative style across products and marketing materials.

### 4. Technology and Sustainability Alignment
- Prefer technologies and suppliers that align with sustainability goals.
- Design hardware and firmware with energy efficiency and recyclability in mind.

### 5. Developer-First Approach
- Provide clear APIs, documentation, and reference implementations.
- Ensure firmware and embedded software follow reproducible build processes and CI/CD practices.

### 6. Security and Privacy by Default
- Apply secure-by-design principles across devices, central hub, and cloud services.
- Treat user data with strict privacy controls and follow GDPR and other applicable regulations.

## How to Use This Document

- Designers: reference the visual identity rules and brand principles when creating assets.
- Engineers: follow the development policies for firmware, embedded Linux, backend services, and CI/CD.
- Marketing: apply messaging guidelines and ensure product claims match technical capabilities.
- Partners: use the brand assets and follow the partnership guidelines when co-branding.
