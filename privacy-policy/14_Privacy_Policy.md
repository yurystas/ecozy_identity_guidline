# 14. Privacy Policy

**Text:** Our Privacy Policy is a public, transparent document that clearly explains to our users *what* data we collect, *why* we collect it, and *how* we use it. Key points include:

1. **Data Collected:**  
   * **User-Provided:** Email, password, name, address.  
   * **System-Collected:** Room temperatures, heating schedules, device telemetry (e.s., battery levels, connectivity), mobile app usage analytics (anonymized).  
2. **Use of Data:**  
   * To provide and maintain the core heating service.  
   * To improve system performance and develop new features (e.g., AI algorithms).  
   * To provide customer support.  
3. **Data Sharing:** We state unequivocally that **we do not sell user data**. We only share data with third-party-service providers (e.g., AWS for hosting) as necessary to operate the service, or if required by law.
